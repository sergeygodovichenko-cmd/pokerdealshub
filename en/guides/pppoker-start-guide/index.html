How to Start Playing PPPoker: Registration, Rakeback and Reliable Unions

PPPoker is one of the most popular platforms for online poker through a closed club system.

How to Start Playing PPPoker

  1. Download the PPPoker app from official website
  2. Contact an agent to get the club ID
  3. Top up your balance and choose a suitable union

PPPoker Rake and Rakeback

Rake is the commission clubs take from each hand. Through agents, players get rakeback:

    1. Beginners — 40-50%
    2. Regulars — up to 60%
    3. Partners — up to 70%+
