Complete PPPoker Guide: Deals, Unions and Rakeback

PPPoker is one of the most popular decentralized poker apps, offering players access to numerous clubs and unions worldwide.

What is PPPoker?

PPPoker is a mobile poker application that operates on a private club principle. Unlike centralized rooms, players join independent clubs that unite into unions.

PPPoker Advantages

    1. High rakeback: up to 60% and higher
    2. Soft tables: thanks to private clubs
    3. Flexible terms: each club sets its own rules
    4. Format variety: NLH, PLO, OFC and more
